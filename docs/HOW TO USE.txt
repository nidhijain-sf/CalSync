====================================
  CalSync by ACE
  Salesforce to Google Calendar Sync
====================================

WHAT THIS APP DOES
------------------
Syncs your Salesforce calendar events (Billable Utilization)
to your Google Calendar automatically every day at 9am.

If your laptop was off at 9am, it will catch up automatically
the next time you open it.


IF YOU ARE ON A MAC
-------------------
IMPORTANT — First time only, macOS will block the installer. Follow these exact steps:

1. Extract the zip and move the "CalSync-Share" folder to your Applications folder
2. Open the "mac" folder
3. Double-click "Install.command"
   -> macOS will show a warning and block it. That is expected. Select "OK".
4. Open System Settings -> Privacy & Security
5. Scroll down — you will see:
   "Install.command was blocked"
6. Select "Open Anyway" -> Select "Open Anyway"
7. Enter your Mac password if prompted
8. The installer runs — your browser opens automatically
9. Connect your Salesforce account
10. Connect your Google Calendar account
    a. Select your Google account
    b. On the "Google hasn't verified this app" screen, select "Advanced"
       -> "Go to Calendar Sync (unsafe)"
    c. On the "Calendar Sync wants access to your Google Account" screen,
       select "Continue"
11. Pick an event color (optional)
12. Click "Sync Now" to run your first sync

That's it! The app runs in the background and syncs every day at 9am.

To uninstall: double-click "Uninstall.command" and follow the same
Privacy & Security steps above if blocked.


IF YOU ARE ON WINDOWS
---------------------
1. Extract the zip and open the "windows" folder
2. Double-click "Install.bat"
   -> The app installs itself and opens in your browser automatically
3. Connect your Salesforce account
4. Connect your Google Calendar account
5. Pick an event color (optional)
6. Click "Sync Now" to run your first sync

That's it! The app runs in the background and syncs every day at 9am.

To uninstall: double-click "Uninstall.bat".
